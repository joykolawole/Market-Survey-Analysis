# 📊 Market Survey Analysis

## 📌 Overview  
This project analyzes customer behavior and preferences using data collected from a structured market survey. The goal is to understand demand patterns, customer satisfaction, and key factors influencing purchasing decisions.

By transforming raw survey responses into meaningful insights, this analysis supports data-driven decisions in product development and marketing strategy.


## 🎯 Objectives  
- Understand customer preferences and demand patterns  
- Analyze satisfaction levels across different segments  
- Identify key factors influencing purchasing decisions  
- Provide actionable recommendations for business improvement  


## 📁 Dataset  
The dataset was collected through a structured survey and includes:
- Customer demographics  
- Product preferences  
- Satisfaction ratings  
- Feedback responses  


## 🧹 Data Cleaning  
- Removed incomplete and duplicate responses  
- Standardized categorical variables  
- Validated and structured survey responses for analysis  


## 🛠️ Tools Used  
- Microsoft Excel  
- Power BI  


## 📈 Analysis & Insights  
- Identified key customer segments based on preferences and behavior  
- Analyzed satisfaction levels to determine strengths and improvement areas  
- Discovered trends in product demand across different customer groups  
- Highlighted factors influencing customer purchasing decisions  


## 📊 Dashboard  
The Power BI dashboard provides:
- Customer segmentation insights  
- Demand distribution across products  
- Satisfaction analysis  
- Interactive filters for deeper exploration
  

## 💡 Key Insights  
- Customer preferences vary significantly across different segments  
- Certain products show consistently higher demand  
- Satisfaction levels highlight areas for potential improvement  
- Data reveals clear patterns in customer behavior and expectations  


## 🚀 Recommendations  
- Focus on high-demand products to maximize revenue  
- Improve areas with low customer satisfaction  
- Tailor marketing strategies to specific customer segments  
- Use customer feedback to guide product development  


## 📉 Business Impact  
This analysis enables businesses to better understand their customers, optimize product offerings, and make informed decisions that improve customer satisfaction and overall performance.
